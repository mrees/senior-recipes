Turkey Roll-Ups Plate

Slices of deli turkey rolled up with cheese, arranged neatly alongside pickles and crackers.

🛒 Ingredients per serving

  • 100 g deli turkey breast slices
  • 30 g Swiss or cheddar cheese slices
  • 4 whole-grain crackers
  • 4 dill pickles or cucumber slices
  • 1 tsp mustard (optional)

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Lay turkey slices flat; place cheese in the centre and roll up.
  2. Arrange rolls alongside crackers and pickles.
  3. Serve with mustard if desired.
