Tofu & Spinach Scramble

Firm tofu crumbled and cooked with spinach and turmeric — a plant-based, protein-rich scramble that is a perfect egg alternative.

🛒 Ingredients per serving

  • 100 g firm tofu
  • Large handful of baby spinach
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Crumble tofu with your hands into rough egg-like pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a small pan over medium heat. Add tofu and turmeric. Cook for 3–4 minutes.
  3. Add spinach, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir until spinach wilts. Serve hot.
