One-Pan Baked Egg Frittata

A fluffy baked frittata made by pouring whisked eggs over spinach, tomatoes and onions.

🛒 Ingredients per serving

  • 3 large eggs
  • 40 g baby spinach
  • 5 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ small onion, finely diced
  • 30 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C.
  2. Heat oil in a small oven-safe frying pan over medium heat. Sauté onion 3 min.
  3. Add spinach; cook until wilted. Add tomatoes.
  4. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper; pour over vegetables.
  5. Scatter feta on top. Transfer to oven.
  6. Bake 12–15 min until eggs are set and top is lightly golden.
