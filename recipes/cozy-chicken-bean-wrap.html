Chicken & Bean Warm Wrap

🍳 5-Minute Meals

A warm wrap filled with cooked chicken and beans — hearty, portable and simple.

Serves
1
Calories
380 kcal
Protein
28g
Fibre
8g

Ingredients

  • 1 small wholegrain tortilla (15 cm)
  • 80 g (3 oz) cooked chicken, shredded
  • 60 ml (¼ cup) canned black or pinto beans, rinsed
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream
  • Pinch of cumin and paprika

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortilla in a dry pan for 30 seconds each side.
  2. Mix chicken, beans and spices in the pan over low heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Spread yogurt over the tortilla.
  4. Add the filling and roll firmly.
