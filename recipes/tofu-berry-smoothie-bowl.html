Tofu & Berry Smoothie Bowl

Silken tofu blended with frozen berries into a thick, creamy smoothie bowl — a high-protein, dairy-free breakfast option.

🛒 Ingredients per serving

  • 100 g silken tofu
  • 120 g frozen mixed berries
  • 60 ml milk or plant-based milk
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Toppings: granola, seeds, or fresh fruit

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Blend tofu, berries, milk, and honey until very smooth and thick.
  2. Pour into a bowl — it should be thick enough to hold toppings.
  3. Add chosen toppings and serve immediately.
