Cabbage and Tofu Stir

🌱 Tofu Simplified

Shredded cabbage steamed soft in a pan with tofu cubes and soy sauce — budget-friendly and filling.

Serves
1
Calories
260 kcal
Protein
14g
Fibre
4g

Ingredients

  • 240 ml (1 cup) chopped cabbage
  • ½ block (150 g) tofu, cubed
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Splash of water

Instructions

  1. Cook cabbage in oiled pan over medium heat 5–7 minutes.
  2. Add a splash of water and cover to steam until very soft.
  3. Add tofu cubes and pour soy sauce over.
  4. Stir gently and cook 4–5 minutes until heated through.
