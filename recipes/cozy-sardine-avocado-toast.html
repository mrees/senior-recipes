Simple Sardine & Avocado Toast

🍳 5-Minute Meals

Mashed avocado and sardines on wholegrain toast — omega-3 rich and ready in minutes.

Serves
1
Calories
340 kcal
Protein
22g
Fibre
5g

Ingredients

  • 1 slice wholegrain bread, toasted
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • 1 small tin sardines in olive oil, drained
  • Squeeze of lemon
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado with lemon juice and a pinch of salt.
  3. Spread avocado over the toast.
  4. Lay sardines on top and finish with black pepper.
