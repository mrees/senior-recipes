High-Protein Warm Pudding

🌅 Gentle Morning Bites

A tiny, warm oat pudding with protein powder and nut butter — big nutrition in a small bowl.

Serves
1
Calories
220 kcal
Protein
12g
Fibre
3g

Ingredients

  • 30 ml (¼ cup) instant oats
  • ½ scoop (15 g) plain or vanilla protein powder
  • 80 ml (⅓ cup) hot water or warm milk
  • 1 tbsp nut butter

Instructions

  1. Combine oats and protein powder in a small bowl.
  2. Pour over hot water or warm milk and stir well.
  3. Let sit 2–3 minutes until thick.
  4. Swirl in nut butter and serve warm.
