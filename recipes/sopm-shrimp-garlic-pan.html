Shrimp & Garlic Pan

🍳 One-Pan Meals

Pre-peeled shrimp cooked quickly in olive oil with garlic — soft, fast and selenium-rich.

Serves
1
Calories
280 kcal
Protein
22g
Fibre
1g

Ingredients

  • 100 g pre-peeled shrimp
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1–2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add shrimp and cook 2–3 minutes on one side until pink.
  3. Flip, add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Season and serve immediately.
