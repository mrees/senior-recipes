Sardine & Tomato Warm Skillet

🍳 One-Pan Meals

Sardines gently warmed in a tomato-garlic sauce — rich in omega-3s and extremely soft.

Serves
1
Calories
290 kcal
Protein
24g
Fibre
3g

Ingredients

  • 1 tin sardines in water or olive oil
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped, or ½ cup canned
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Squeeze of lemon

Instructions

  1. Sauté tomato and garlic in olive oil 3–4 minutes until broken down.
  2. Gently add sardines — do not over-stir.
  3. Let them warm through for 2 minutes.
  4. Finish with lemon. Serve over soft rice or mashed potato.
