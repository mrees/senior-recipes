Soft Tofu Vegetable Soup

A comforting bowl of broth with sliced carrots, silken tofu, soy sauce and fresh spinach.

🛒 Ingredients per serving

  • 150 g silken tofu, cubed
  • 400 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 medium carrot (≈80 g), thinly sliced
  • 40 g baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 spring onion, sliced

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Bring stock to a simmer in a saucepan. Add carrot; cook 5 min.
  2. Add silken tofu cubes; simmer gently 3 min.
  3. Stir in spinach and soy sauce; cook 1 min until spinach wilts.
  4. Drizzle sesame oil; top with spring onion.
