The Quick High-Fiber Smoothie

🌿 No-Cook Fiber Meals

A blended drink with milk, banana, frozen berries, flax seed and fresh spinach.

Serves
1
Calories
280 kcal
Protein
10g
Fibre
6g

Ingredients

  • 240 ml (1 cup) milk of choice
  • ½ medium banana
  • 120 ml (½ cup) frozen berries
  • 1 tbsp ground flax seed
  • 120 ml (½ cup) fresh spinach

Instructions

  1. Pour milk into the blender first.
  2. Add banana, berries, flax seed and spinach.
  3. Blend 30 seconds until completely smooth.
  4. Stir in 1 tsp chia seeds after blending if desired.
