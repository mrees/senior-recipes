Soft Tofu Vegetable Soup

🌱 Tofu Simplified

Silken tofu simmered gently in a light vegetable broth with carrots and spinach.

Serves
1
Calories
220 kcal
Protein
12g
Fibre
3g

Ingredients

  • ½ block (150 g) silken tofu
  • 240 ml (1 cup) vegetable broth
  • 60 ml (¼ cup) sliced carrots
  • Handful of fresh spinach
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Warm broth in a small pot over medium heat.
  2. Add carrots and cook 5 minutes until tender.
  3. Lower heat. Gently spoon in silken tofu chunks.
  4. Add soy sauce and spinach. Simmer 3–5 minutes — never boil.
