Warm Egg, Spinach & White Bean Skillet

🍳 5-Minute Meals

A warm, protein-rich skillet of eggs, spinach and white beans ready in five minutes.

Serves
1
Calories
340 kcal
Protein
24g
Fibre
8g

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 120 ml (½ cup) canned white beans, rinsed
  • 1 large handful fresh spinach
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper and a pinch of garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a small pan over medium heat.
  2. Add beans and spinach and cook 2 minutes until spinach wilts.
  3. Create two small wells and crack an egg into each.
  4. Cover and cook 2–3 minutes until whites are set. Season and serve.
