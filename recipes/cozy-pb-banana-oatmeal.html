Warm PB Banana Oatmeal

🍳 5-Minute Meals

Creamy porridge with peanut butter and banana — warm, comforting and ready quickly.

Serves
1
Calories
360 kcal
Protein
12g
Fibre
6g

Ingredients

  • 60 ml (¼ cup) rolled oats
  • 180 ml (¾ cup) milk or water
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • ½ medium banana, sliced
  • Pinch of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Combine oats and milk in a small pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook 3–4 minutes, stirring, until thick and creamy.
  3. Stir in the peanut butter.
  4. Top with banana slices and a pinch of cinnamon.
