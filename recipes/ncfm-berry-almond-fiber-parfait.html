The Berry Almond Fiber Parfait

🌿 No-Cook Fiber Meals

Greek yogurt layered with fresh raspberries, high-fibre granola and sliced almonds.

Serves
1
Calories
290 kcal
Protein
16g
Fibre
8g

Ingredients

  • 180 ml (¾ cup) plain Greek yogurt
  • 120 ml (½ cup) fresh raspberries
  • 2 tbsp high-fibre granola
  • 1 tbsp sliced almonds
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions

  1. Spoon yogurt into the bottom of a glass or bowl.
  2. Spread raspberries evenly on top.
  3. Sprinkle granola and almonds over the fruit.
  4. Drizzle with honey and serve immediately.
